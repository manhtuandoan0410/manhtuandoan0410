Welcome to the technical readout for Project Ares A-1. This site documents a bio-cybernetic guardian—an integrated human brain (as the ethical core) fused with a reinforced cybernetic chassis. The design emphasizes redundancy, safety, and survivability while pushing near-future technologies (BCI, DEAs, SSBs).

Executive Summary

  • Mandate: Protect the designer's family and maintain continuity of care via Neural Backup & Restoration Protocols (NBRP).
  • Composition: 2-meter, 450 kg hybrid construct combining graphene/titanium composites, dielectric elastomer actuators (DEAs), and distributed solid-state batteries (BESS).
  • Control: Prometheus COM with Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) for instant intent translation; ethical constraints enforced by an Ethical Constraint Layer (ECL).
  • Resilience: Internal Repair Module (IRM) housing Cyber-Bots for autonomous additive repairs; multi-layer smart armor with STF and self-healing polymers.
